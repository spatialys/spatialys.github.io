Services

Spatialys is in the position of offering the following services to its clients:

  • improving existing functionnality or developing new functionnality in GDAL/OGR or MapServer, following the development guidelines of those projects and their quality process,
  • designing, writing and enhancing translators for new formats in GDAL/OGR,
  • analyzing performance issues, and implementing enhancements,
  • fixing specific issues they might run into,
  • polishing their own contributed patches for inclusion in the upstream projects,
  • providing assistance to integrate GDAL/OGR or MapServer into their own software and infrastructure,
  • providing broader consulting and expertise on issues related to geospatial data.
  • training at all levels on GDAL/OGR.

And for any other need you may have, related to the above topics, please contact us
